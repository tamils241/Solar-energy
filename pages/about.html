10+ Years Experience

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Stackly Solar Energy began with a simple mission: make clean, renewable energy accessible to every home and business. What started as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into a premier solar provider with thousands of successful installations across the country.

We believe the transition to solar energy is not just an environmental responsibility but a smart financial decision. Our innovative financing models and cutting-edge technology have helped families and businesses save millions on energy costs while drastically reducing their carbon footprint.

Today, Stackly Solar employs over 200 dedicated professionals and has installed more than 5,000 solar systems. With a 98% customer satisfaction rate, we're proud to be one of the most trusted names in renewable energy.
